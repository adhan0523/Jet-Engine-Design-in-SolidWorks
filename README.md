## Overview

This repository documents my journey of designing a **Jet Engine (Turbofan)** in SolidWorks.

Rather than modeling the entire engine in a single session, I am designing it component by component to better understand the structure and functionality of each section. This approach allows me to focus on the design details while steadily building a complete engine assembly.

The repository serves as both a learning journal and a portfolio project, showcasing my progress as I develop the model from individual parts to a fully assembled jet engine.

---

## Why I Started This Project

After completing several CAD projects, I wanted to take on something that was both challenging and rewarding. A jet engine stood out because of its intricate geometry, numerous components, and real-world engineering significance.

This project gives me the opportunity to explore advanced modeling techniques while gaining a better understanding of how different engine components work together to produce thrust.

---

* Design a complete jet engine assembly in SolidWorks.

## Current Progress

This project is currently under development.

To stay consistent, I design **one or two components each day**, refining them before moving on to the next stage of the assembly. Working this way helps me focus on accuracy and gives me time to understand the function of every component instead of simply recreating its appearance.

As more components are completed, they will be assembled into the final jet engine model and documented in this repository.
